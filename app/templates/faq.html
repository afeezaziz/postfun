FAQ

Is this real trading?

No. The app uses mock data for charts, holders, and swaps. The trade button adjusts price slightly and shows a success message.

How do I login?

Click "Login with Nostr" in the header. Your NIP-07 extension signs an auth event. The server verifies, sets an HttpOnly cookie, and the template renders your user.

Where is my data stored?

Users and tokens are stored in the configured database (MariaDB/SQLite fallback). JWT cookies are HttpOnly and not accessible via JS.
